<Section icon={Shield} title="Co-op Strategies for Dungeon Success">
Multiplayer coordination is at the heart of many challenging **Embers of the Uncrowned dungeon** experiences. Whether you're reclaiming a domain or tackling a raid, effective teamwork will determine your success. The demo includes multiplayer-focused field and matchmaking content across three core regions and 10 explorable or liberatable regions.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Understand Your Role">
    Before entering any dungeon, ensure each player understands their class role (DPS, Tank, Healer) and specific responsibilities during boss encounters. **Effective role distribution** is vital for raid groups.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Communicate Constantly">
    Use in-game chat or external voice communication to call out mechanics, warn teammates of incoming attacks, and coordinate skill usage. **Clear communication** prevents wipes.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Master Evasion and Interrupts">
    Manual combat emphasizes precise evasion and interrupt timing. Practice dodging boss abilities and saving your interrupt skills for critical moments to **prevent devastating attacks**.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Optimize Party Synergy">
    Combine class skills with companion effects for powerful synergies. For example, a Stormbringer's elemental pressure can be amplified by a companion providing crowd control or damage buffs. **Strategic companion use** enhances overall party strength.
  </Step>
  <Step num="5" title="Adapt to Boss Patterns">
    Each boss has unique attack patterns and phases. Be prepared to adapt your strategy on the fly, focusing on survival during dangerous phases and maximizing damage during vulnerability windows. **Flexibility is key**.
  </Step>
</Steps>
