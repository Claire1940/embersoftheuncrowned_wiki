Embers of the Uncrowned features three distinct playable classes in its demo: Executioner, Spectral Blade, and Stormbringer. Each offers a unique combat experience and progression path. Understanding their roles and core mechanics is crucial for effective character creation.

<CardGrid cols={3}>

    - **Core Mechanics**: Builds Rage, triggers Fury for explosive tempo and skill chains.
    - **Elements**: Physical and Fire
    - **Difficulty**: Medium
    - **Best For**: Players who prefer durable melee combat, clear resource flow, and heavy burst damage.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Stormbringer">
    - **Role**: Wind and lightning magic damage dealer
    - **Core Mechanics**: Uses Wind and Lightning to control space and deliver magical pressure from safer positions.
    - **Elements**: Wind and Lightning
    - **Difficulty**: Medium
    - **Best For**: Players who prefer ranged magic, elemental burst, and a clearer view of battlefield patterns.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Spectral Blade">
    - **Role**: Mobile melee assassin and phantom duelist
    - **Core Mechanics**: Generates and consumes Shade, chains skills with Phantoms, uses Stealth and position swaps.

    - **Difficulty**: High

<Section icon={SlidersHorizontal} title="Initial Customization and Progression">
Beyond the initial class choice, character creation in Embers of the Uncrowned extends into how you develop your character through skills, gear, and bloodline progression. This continuous customization allows for deep build diversity.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Choose a Demo Class">
    Select from Stormbringer, Executioner, or Spectral Blade. Each offers a distinct playstyle: Stormbringer for ranged magic, Executioner for heavy melee, and Spectral Blade for agile close-range combat.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Learn Active and Passive Skill Progression">
    Each class has 28-29 skills, divided into active and passive systems. Active skills define your combat choices, while passive skills support your class identity and survivability.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Match Skills to Class Identity">
    Your skill choices should align with your class's core attributes. Stormbringers focus on magical burst, Executioners on high-impact melee, and Spectral Blades on speed and combos.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Layer Skill Gems and Gear Enhancement">
    Character growth involves combining **Skill Gems**, enhancing gear, and awakening **bloodline power**. These layers contribute significantly to your combat power.
  </Step>
  <Step num="5" title="Push Progression Through Demo Content">
    The demo offers three main story chapters, 34 side quests, and over 480 minutes of content to explore progression systems, including region liberation, boss content, and companion growth.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="success" title="Early Game Build Focus">
For the demo, focus on understanding your chosen class's primary damage output and defensive mechanics. Experiment with different skill combinations to find what feels most effective against various enemy types and bosses.
</Callout>
