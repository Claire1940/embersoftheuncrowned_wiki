<Section icon={Sparkles} title="Progression: Skill Gems and Bloodlines">

Power in **embers of the uncrowned combat** is not just about your level; it is about how you layer your progression systems. The "Infinite Progression" model allows players to customize their combat style through three main pillars: Skill Gems, Gear Enhancement, and Bloodline Awakening.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Select Active Skills">
    Choose 6 active skills from your class's pool of 28. Balance between quick resource generators and high-damage spenders.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Socket Skill Gems">
    Apply Skill Gems to your active abilities. These can change a skill's element, reduce cooldowns, or add utility like "Stun" or "Lifesteal."
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Enhance Combat Gear">
    Use resources gathered from Domain Bosses to upgrade your weapons and armor, increasing base Physical and Elemental resistance.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Awaken Bloodline Power">
    Unlock the latent power of House Harborwell. This "Ultimate" state provides temporary buffs to speed, damage, and resource regeneration.
  </Step>
</Steps>

Skill Gems are the most transformative part of the combat system. A Stormbringer's "Chain Lightning" can be modified with a "Gale Gem" to push enemies back, or a "Thunder Gem" to increase critical strike chance against staggered targets. This level of customization ensures that two players of the same class can have vastly different combat roles within a raid.

| Progression Layer | Primary Source | Combat Impact |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Skill Gems** | World Drops / Raids | Modifies skill behavior and elements |
| **Gear Enhancement** | Crafting / Settlement | Increases raw HP, MP, and Attack |
| **Bloodline Power** | Story Chapters | Provides a powerful "Awakening" buff |
| **Companion Skills** | Eslyn Affinity | Adds passive support or active synergy |

<Section icon={Shield} title="Tactical Co-op: Bosses and Raids">

The 2026 demo highlights 12 boss monsters across various encounter types. Success in these high-level encounters requires more than just high DPS; it requires "Combat Synergy" with your companions and party members. Domain Bosses require players to reclaim land for House Harborwell, while Raid Bosses are designed for high-coordination group play.

The "Stagger Window" is the most important mechanic to master. When a boss prepares a powerful attack, a yellow or purple bar appears. Using "Interrupt" skills during this time will deplete the bar. Once empty, the boss is Staggered, taking 200% increased damage for a short duration.

| Encounter Type | Player Count | Difficulty | Primary Objective |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Field Boss** | Open World | Low / Medium | Group up to clear regional threats |
| **Domain Boss** | 1 - 4 Players | Medium | Reclaim territory for the Settlement |
| **Defense of Spring** | 4 Players | High | Defend a central objective from waves |
| **Raid Encounter** | 4+ Players | Very High | Use coordinated interrupts and tactics |
| **PvP Arena** | 1v1 / 3v3 | Competitive | Master positioning and skill timing |

Companions like **Eslyn** also play a role in these fights. With 17 companion skills available in the demo, you can set your ally to focus on healing, providing shields, or assisting with interrupts. Building "Affinity" with Eslyn through quests unlocks more powerful combat synergies that bolster your overall force.

<Callout type="info" title="Raid Strategy">
In 4-player raids, assign one player (usually an Executioner) to save their "Interrupt" skill exclusively for the boss's wipe mechanic. Do not waste these skills on standard attacks.
</Callout>
