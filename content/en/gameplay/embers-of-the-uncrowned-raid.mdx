<Section icon={Shield} title="Raid Mechanics: Evasion and Interrupts">

The core of the **embers of the uncrowned raid** experience is manual combat. The game does not feature "auto-combat" for these high-tier encounters. Players must actively watch the boss's animations to survive.

**Interrupting Boss Skills**
Many bosses in the demo, especially in the Towerstead region, utilize "Purple" or "Red" telegraphed attacks.
- **Purple Attacks**: These can usually be interrupted by specific skills, putting the boss into a short stun.
- **Red Attacks**: These are typically unblockable and require precise evasion or moving out of the area of effect (AoE).

| Mechanic | Player Action | Result of Success | Penalty for Failure |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Interrupt** | Use skill with Interrupt tag | Boss stun / Stagger gain | Heavy party damage |
| **Perfect Dodge** | Evade at the last moment | SP recovery / Counter window | Direct HP loss |
| **Stagger** | Continuous heavy pressure | Massive damage window | Extended fight duration |

<Callout type="info" title="Bloodline Awakening">
Don't forget to activate your Bloodline power during the Stagger window. This inherited power provides a massive temporary boost to all combat stats, making it the ideal tool for pushing through a boss's final health phase.
</Callout>

</Section>
