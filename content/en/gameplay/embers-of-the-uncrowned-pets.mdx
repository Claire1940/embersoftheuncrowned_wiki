<Section icon={Swords} title="Companion Skills and Combat Mechanics">

Companions in *Embers of the Uncrowned* are designed to be an extension of your own build. The demo features a robust skill system for your allies, boasting 17 companion skills that can be unlocked and customized. These skills allow your companion to fill various tactical roles, such as a defensive tank, a crowd-control specialist, or a high-damage support.

When engaging in high-difficulty content like Domain Bosses, Field Bosses, or cooperative raids, your companion's skill loadout can mean the difference between victory and defeat. For example, a companion set up with crowd-control skills can stagger bosses, creating crucial burst-damage windows for classes like the Executioner or Spectral Blade.

| Skill Category | Primary Focus | Best Use Case | Tactical Benefit |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Vanguard** | Threat generation and defense | Solo play for squishy ranged classes | Keeps enemies away from Stormbringer casters |
| **Tactician** | Crowd control and stagger | Domain Boss and Raid encounters | Interrupts dangerous boss skills and creates openings |
| **Apothecary** | Healing and shield support | High-difficulty cooperative content | Reduces reliance on health potions during long fights |
| **Striker** | Elemental and physical DPS | Fast-paced solo farming routes | Speeds up region clearing and daily quests |

<Callout type="success" title="Combat Synergy Tip">
Always match your companion's active skills to your character's elemental build. If you are playing a Fire-based Executioner, equipping your companion with defense-shredding or burn-amplifying skills will exponentially increase your total damage output.

<Section icon={Target} title="Unlocking Companions & Building Affinity">

Unlocking and upgrading your companions requires active participation in both the main campaign and the settlement systems. To maximize your companion's potential, you must progress through their exclusive affinity-based questlines. This process not only reveals their tragic backstories but also unlocks powerful passive abilities that bolster your entire party.

In the 2026 demo, companion progression is directly linked to the Amber Coast Settlement and House Harborwell. As you upgrade settlement buildings and liberate surrounding regions from elven forces, you will unlock new ways to interact with your allies, research companion-specific upgrades, and craft high-tier companion gear.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Complete early story chapters in Goldenhaven">
    Progress through the main scenario quests in Goldenhaven to officially recruit Eslyn. This unlocks the companion interface and your first set of companion skills.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Establish the Amber Coast Settlement">
    Reclaim the fallen domain of House Harborwell and upgrade your camp into a functional settlement. This unlocks companion training facilities and crafting benches.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Initiate Eslyn's affinity quests">
    Speak with Eslyn in the settlement to accept exclusive affinity-based questlines. Completing these quests increases her bond level and unlocks advanced combat skills.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Upgrade settlement research and trade facilities">
    Build and upgrade the seven available settlement buildings in the demo. Use these facilities to research companion passive boosts and trade for rare skill gems.
  </Step>
</Steps>

By coordinating your active skills, passive progression, and skill gems with your companion's loadout, you can create highly efficient builds capable of tackling the game's toughest cooperative challenges.

  <Card title="Spectral Blade Pairings">
