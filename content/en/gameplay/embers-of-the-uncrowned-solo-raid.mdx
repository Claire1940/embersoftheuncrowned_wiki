<Section icon={Sparkles} title="Skill Gems, Gear Enhancement, and Bloodline Progression">
Infinite Progression in Embers of the Uncrowned involves a complex layering of Skill Gems, gear enhancement, and bloodline awakening. For solo raid attempts, maximizing these systems is not optional—it's a requirement. Each layer contributes significantly to your character's combat power and ability to withstand and overcome powerful bosses.

### Progression Layering for Solo Success

| Progression Layer | Impact on Solo Raids | Optimization Tips |
|:------------------|:---------------------|:------------------|
| **Skill Gems**    | Adaptability, specific stat boosts | Prioritize gems that enhance your primary damage, provide defensive buffs, or reduce cooldowns. |
| **Gear Enhancement**| Raw power increase, set bonuses | Focus on upgrading weapons and armor for higher base stats. Seek out gear with beneficial affixes. |
| **Bloodline Awakening**| Unique passive bonuses, stat scaling | Unlock bloodline nodes that complement your class build, offering sustained damage or survivability. |
| **Active Skills** | Combat rhythm, burst windows | Specialize in skills that offer high single-target damage or reliable AoE for add management. |
| **Passive Skills** | Core identity, survivability | Invest in passives that boost your chosen damage type, increase health, or provide defensive mechanisms. |

<Callout type="success" title="Synergize Your Systems">
The true power of your build comes from synergizing these progression layers. For example, a Skill Gem that boosts fire damage will be more effective if your gear also has fire damage affixes and your bloodline awakening enhances elemental effects.
</Callout>
