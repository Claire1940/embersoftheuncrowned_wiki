<Section icon={Sparkles} title="Optimizing Your Build for PvP Success">
Optimizing your character build is critical for consistent success in **Embers of the Uncrowned PvP**. This involves carefully selecting skills, Skill Gems, gear enhancements, and bloodline progression to create a cohesive and powerful combat profile.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Prioritize PvP-Oriented Skills">
    Review your class's active and passive skills. Focus on abilities that offer crowd control, burst damage, mobility, or defensive utility against other players. PvE-focused skills that deal sustained damage to single targets might be less effective in the fast-paced PvP environment.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Select Strategic Skill Gems">
    Skill Gems are a core part of character growth. Choose gems that enhance your chosen PvP playstyle. For example, a Spectral Blade might prioritize gems that increase Shade generation or burst damage from stealth, while a Stormbringer might focus on gems that boost elemental damage or reduce cast times.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Enhance Gear for Survivability and Damage">
    Gear enhancement provides another layer of power. Balance defensive stats (HP, resistances) with offensive stats (attack power, critical chance) to ensure you can both withstand enemy attacks and deal significant damage. Specific gear sets might also offer unique bonuses beneficial for PvP.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Leverage Bloodline Awakening">
    Bloodline awakening connects your inherited power to late-game progression. Invest in bloodline traits that complement your PvP build, whether it's increased resilience, enhanced skill effects, or faster resource regeneration.
  </Step>
  <Step num="5" title="Practice and Adapt">
    The best build is one that you can effectively wield. Practice your rotations, evasion techniques, and target prioritization in the demo's multiplayer content. Analyze your losses to identify weaknesses in your build or strategy and adapt accordingly.
  </Step>
</Steps>
