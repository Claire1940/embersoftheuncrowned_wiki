<Section icon={Swords} title="Combat Mechanics: Evasion, Interrupts, and Stagger Windows">

Manual isometric combat is the core pillar of the combat system. To defeat any high-tier boss, you must look beyond basic attack spam and master the active defensive options built into the engine. Bosses possess massive health pools and devastating area-of-effect (AoE) attacks that can easily wipe an unprepared party.

The combat flow relies on three primary mechanics: precise evasion, skill interrupts, and executing damage during stagger windows. Evasion consumes Stamina Points (SP), meaning you cannot dodge indefinitely. Managing this resource is critical during prolonged encounters.

| Mechanic | Resource Cost | Visual Indicator | Strategic Purpose |
|---|---|---|---|
| **Evasion** | Medium SP | Character dash animation | Avoid lethal AoE patterns and reposition behind the boss |
| **Interrupt** | Skill Cooldown | Blue or yellow casting bar | Break dangerous boss skills and prevent wipe mechanics |
| **Stagger** | None | Depleting posture/stagger bar | Create a high-damage burst window with critical modifiers |

To successfully handle a boss wipe mechanic, follow this standard tactical sequence:

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Identify the Cast Bar">
    Watch the boss's overhead casting bar. A glowing blue or yellow outline indicates an interruptible spell that will deal massive party-wide damage if completed.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Deploy Crowd Control (CC) Skills">
    Coordinate with your team to unleash heavy CC or specialized interrupt skills. Executioners should lead with shock-inducing strikes, while Stormbringers apply elemental pressure.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Expose the Stagger Window">
    Once the interrupt succeeds, the boss's stagger bar will deplete rapidly. Hit the boss with high-impact skills to trigger the staggered state, rendering them defenseless.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Unleash Burst Damage">
    With the boss staggered, swap to high-damage modifiers. Spectral Blades should utilize Rear Strike bonuses, while Stormbringers cast their longest cooldown elemental spells.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="tip" title="Stamina Management">
Never exhaust your SP bar entirely on minor attacks. Always keep at least one dodge charge available to escape sudden ground-targeted hazards or boss charge attacks.
</Callout>

</Section>
