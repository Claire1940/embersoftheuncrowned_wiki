<Section icon={Swords} title="Class Mobility and Mount Synergy">

While **embers of the uncrowned mounts** provide the best way to travel between regions, your chosen class determines how you move *during* combat. The synergy between your mount and your class's innate mobility is vital for tackling the 12 boss monsters featured in the demo.

- **Spectral Blade**: This class is the master of short-range mobility. Using Phantoms and Stealth, the Spectral Blade can reposition instantly, making a mount less necessary for micro-travel but essential for moving between distant quest objectives.
- **Executioner**: As a heavy melee vanguard, the Executioner lacks the swiftness of the Spectral Blade. For this class, a mount is a critical tool to close the gap between major settlement hubs and front-line battles.
- **Stormbringer**: The Stormbringer uses wind magic to control space. While they have ranged pressure, their defensive movement is limited, making the Cloudfur Cottonsloth an excellent "get-away" tool when exploring contested regions.

| Class Name | Combat Mobility | Travel Dependency | Role |

| **Spectral Blade** | Very High | Moderate | Assassin/Duelist |
| **Executioner** | Moderate | High | Heavy Melee/Vanguard |
| **Stormbringer** | Low | High | Ranged Caster |
