<Section icon={Settings} title="Setting Up Your Controller for Embers of the Uncrowned">
Configuring your controller for Embers of the Uncrowned is straightforward, but optimizing your keybinds can significantly enhance your gameplay experience. The game is designed to be responsive, and a well-mapped controller can provide a tactical advantage in various combat scenarios.

**Controller Setup Steps:**

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Connect Your Controller">
    Ensure your Xbox controller (or compatible gamepad) is properly connected to your PC before launching Embers of the Uncrowned. The game is known to detect standard XInput devices automatically.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Launch the Game">
    Start Embers of the Uncrowned (or the demo via Steam). The game should recognize your controller upon startup. If not, check your Steam Big Picture mode settings or controller drivers.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Access Control Options">
    Navigate to the in-game settings menu. Look for a "Controls" or "Keybindings" section. Here you'll find options to customize your controller layout.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Customize Keybindings">
    Map your active skills, evasion, potions, and bloodline abilities to easily accessible buttons. Prioritize frequently used actions for quick reactions during combat. Consider grouping offensive skills on one side and defensive/utility skills on the other.
  </Step>
  <Step num="5" title="Test Your Setup">
    Enter a low-threat area or the training ground to test your new controller setup. Practice movement, basic attacks, skill combos, and evasion to ensure comfort and responsiveness.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Section icon={Swords} title="Combat Mechanics with Controller Input">
The combat in Embers of the Uncrowned is dynamic and requires precise input, making controller play a highly engaging option. Understanding how evasion, interrupts, and skill chains work with a gamepad is key to excelling.

**Key Combat Elements for Controller Players:**

| Combat Mechanic      | Controller Input Focus                                                               | Player Benefit                                                                    |
| :------------------- | :----------------------------------------------------------------------------------- | :-------------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| **Basic Attacks**    | Consistent rhythm, directional aiming with left stick                                | Maintain damage pressure, build resources for skills                              |
| **Active Skills**    | Quick access to mapped buttons (e.g., face buttons, triggers)                        | Execute timely abilities, initiate combos, exploit enemy weaknesses               |
| **Evasion (Dodge)**  | Dedicated dodge button (e.g., B/Circle), precise directional input                   | Avoid incoming damage, reposition for tactical advantage, conserve health/potions |
| **Interrupts**       | Fast reaction to visual cues, specific skill activation                              | Prevent dangerous boss abilities, create openings for burst damage                |
| **Stagger Windows**  | Recognize visual/audio cues, unleash high-damage skills during vulnerability         | Maximize damage output, shorten boss encounters                                   |
| **Bloodline Abilities** | Dedicated button or combo (e.g., L1+R1), strategic activation                       | Unleash powerful temporary buffs or attacks, turn the tide of battle              |
