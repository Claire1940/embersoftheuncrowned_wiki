<Section icon={Target} title="Core Mechanics and Skill Progression">

The Spectral Blade's combat identity is deeply rooted in its resource management and unique skill interactions. Understanding Shade, Phantoms, and how to weave them into seamless attack patterns is fundamental.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Shade Generation and Consumption">
    Shade is the Spectral Blade's primary resource. It's generated through basic attacks and certain skills, and consumed by powerful finishing moves. Efficient Shade management ensures a consistent flow of high-damage abilities. Focus on skills that both generate and consume Shade to maintain your combat rhythm.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Phantom Chaining">
    Phantoms are spectral duplicates that mirror your attacks or provide additional effects. Chaining skills with Phantoms is crucial for maximizing damage output and extending combos. Look for opportunities to summon Phantoms and synchronize your attacks with them for increased impact.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Stealth and Positional Advantages">
    Stealth is not just for avoiding detection; it's a powerful tool for initiating combat with a burst of damage. Many Spectral Blade skills gain bonus effects when used from Stealth or from specific positions (e.g., Rear Strike). Mastering positional play turns every encounter into a tactical puzzle.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Skill Gems and Gear Enhancement">
    Progression extends beyond raw stats. Skill Gems provide unique modifiers to your abilities, allowing for significant customization. Prioritize Gems that enhance your core damage skills, Shade generation, or Phantom interactions. Gear enhancement further amplifies your chosen build, ensuring your equipment scales with your growing power.
  </Step>
</Steps>
