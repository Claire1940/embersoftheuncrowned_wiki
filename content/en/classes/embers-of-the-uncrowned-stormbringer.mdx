<Section icon={Swords} title="Core Combat Mechanics and Elemental Synergy">

The combat loop for the **embers of the uncrowned stormbringer** is built around the synergy between Wind and Lightning. In the manual isometric combat system, you cannot simply stand still and cast; you must utilize the "Evasion" mechanic, which consumes SP, to avoid lethal boss patterns while maintaining your magical rotation.

### The Dual Force System
The Stormbringer does not just deal damage; it controls space. Wind skills are primarily used to group enemies or push back aggressive melee units, while Lightning skills act as the "finisher" to exploit the openings created.

| Magic Type | Primary Effect | Best Used For |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Wind** | Displacement/Slow | Crowd control, kiting, and creating distance. |
| **Lightning** | High Burst/Shock | Boss damage, defense shredding, and critical hits. |

In the demo, you will encounter 12 boss monsters across Domain, Field, and Raid encounters. The **embers of the uncrowned stormbringer** excels in these scenarios by providing "Interrupt" utility and "Stagger" buildup.

### Handling Domain Bosses
Domain Bosses require you to reclaim land for House Harborwell. As a Stormbringer, your role is to stay at maximum range and use Wind skills to push away "adds" (smaller enemies) while focusing Lightning on the boss.

| Boss Type | Stormbringer Strategy | Difficulty |
