The demo provides roughly 480 minutes of content, which is ample time to experiment with different Skill Gem combinations. These gems act as the primary "tuning" mechanism for your build, allowing you to convert a standard physical attack into a specialized elemental strike or increase the stagger potential of your primary combo.

| Progression Layer | Function | Primary Benefit | Source |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Skill Tree** | Unlock active/passive skills | Defines core combat style | Leveling up |
| **Skill Gems** | Augment existing skills | Adds elemental or utility effects | World drops, Crafting |

| **Bloodline** | Awakening abilities | Unlocks high-tier power spikes | Story progression |

<Section icon={Sparkles} title="Optimizing Your Build for Raids and Bosses">

The endgame of the current demo focuses on 12 boss monsters spread across Domain, Field, and Raid encounters. To succeed in these high-coordination environments, your **embers of the uncrowned build** must account for multiplayer synergy. While solo builds focus on self-sustain, raid builds should prioritize defense shredding and crowd control.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Define Your Role">
    Decide if your build is a primary damage dealer, a stagger specialist, or a support-hybrid using companion skills.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Equip Interrupt Skills">
    Ensure at least two slots are dedicated to skills with high "Interrupt" or "Stagger" values to break boss ultimates.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Sync with Companions">
    Match your elemental affinity with your companion (like Eslyn) to trigger cross-class synergies.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Upgrade Settlement Facilities">
    Use the 7 upgradeable buildings in House Harborwell to research better potions and gear enhancements before a raid.
  </Step>
</Steps>

| Encounter Type | Team Size | Build Focus | Key Reward |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Domain Boss** | Open Co-op | Area Liberation | Territory growth |
| **Raid Boss** | Coordinated Group | Tactical Execution | Legendary Skill Gems |
| **Defense of Spring** | PvE Group | Wave Clear / Sustain | Crafting materials |
| **PvP Arena** | Matchmaking | Mobility / Burst | Competitive ranking |

<Callout type="tip" title="Tactical Advice">
During Domain Boss fights, use "Rear Strikes" if you are playing a **Spectral Blade**. The damage multiplier for attacking from behind is significant and can often be the difference between a successful clear and a wipe.
</Callout>
