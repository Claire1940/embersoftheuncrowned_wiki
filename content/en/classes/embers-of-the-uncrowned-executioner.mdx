### Executioner Core Mechanics

The Executioner's power fantasy is built around two primary mechanics: Rage and Fury. These systems dictate the flow of combat and define the class's burst potential.

| Mechanic | Description                                                                    | Combat Impact                                                                         |
| :------- | :----------------------------------------------------------------------------- | :------------------------------------------------------------------------------------ |
| **Rage**   | Generated by attacking and taking damage. Fills a resource bar.              | Fuels skills and builds towards the powerful Fury state.                              |
| **Fury**   | Triggered when Rage bar is full. Grants significant buffs and unlocks abilities. | Enables explosive burst damage, enhanced pursuit, and extended skill chains.          |
| **Pursuit**| Skills allowing the Executioner to quickly close gaps to enemies.              | Essential for maintaining uptime on targets and maximizing Rage generation.           |
| **Skill Chains** | Specific sequences of abilities that flow together for maximum impact.     | Optimizes damage output and resource management during Fury windows.                  |

Efficiently generating and consuming Rage is the cornerstone of high-performance Executioner play.

| Tactic                        | Description                                                                                             | Benefit                                                                 |
| :---------------------------- | :------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------ | :---------------------------------------------------------------------- |

| **Fury Management**           | Time your Fury activation to coincide with boss vulnerability windows or large enemy pulls.               | Maximizes burst damage and minimizes wasted Fury uptime.                |
| **Skill Interruption**        | Use specific **Executioner** skills to interrupt dangerous enemy abilities, maintaining control.          | Prevents incoming damage and creates openings for your team.            |
| **Positioning for Cleave**    | Position yourself to hit multiple enemies with cleave abilities, especially during Fury.                  | Greatly increases AoE damage and speeds up clear times.                 |
