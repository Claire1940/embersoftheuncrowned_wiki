### Progression Through Skills, Gems, and Bloodline Power
Character progression is multi-layered, evolving from basic class skills to complex systems like Skill Gems and Bloodline Awakening.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Choose a Demo Class">
    The Steam Next Fest demo allows players to experience the **Stormbringer**, **Executioner**, and **Spectral Blade** classes. Each class offers a unique combat rhythm and build direction from the outset.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Learn Active and Passive Skills">
    Each class has 28-29 skills, divided into active and passive systems. Active skills define your moment-to-moment combat choices, while passive progression supports class identity, survivability, and damage style.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Match Skills to Class Identity">
    Tailor your skill choices to your chosen class's strengths. For instance, **Stormbringers** excel with magical burst, **Executioners** with high-impact melee, and **Spectral Blades** with agile combos.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Layer Skill Gems and Gear Enhancement">
    Infinite Progression is centered on combining **Skill Gems**, enhancing gear, and awakening bloodline power. Skill Gems are a long-term growth layer, gear enhancement provides combat power development, and bloodline awakening connects inherited power to late-game progression.
  </Step>
  <Step num="5" title="Push Progression Through Demo Content">
    The demo offers three main story chapters, 34 side quests, and over 480 minutes of content. This allows players to explore region liberation, boss content, and companion growth, all contributing to the wider House Harborwell progression loop.
  </Step>
</Steps>
