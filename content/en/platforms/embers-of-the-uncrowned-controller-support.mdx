  <Step num="1" title="Connect Your Controller">

  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Launch the Game">
    Start Embers of the Uncrowned (or its demo) through Steam. The game should automatically detect your connected controller.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Access Control Settings">

  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Customize Keybindings (If Available)">

  </Step>
  <Step num="5" title="Test Your Setup">
    Enter a low-threat area or the training ground (if available in the full game or demo) to test your controller setup. Practice movement, basic attacks, skill usage, and evasion to ensure comfort and responsiveness.

<Section icon={Shield} title="Controller vs. Keyboard & Mouse: Combat Advantages">

Embers of the Uncrowned's isometric combat system can be played effectively with both keyboard/mouse and controller. Each input method offers distinct advantages, catering to different player preferences.

| Feature             | Controller Advantage                                   | Keyboard & Mouse Advantage                             |
| :------------------ | :----------------------------------------------------- | :----------------------------------------------------- |
| **Movement**        | Analog stick for smooth, omnidirectional movement      | WASD for precise directional movement                  |
| **Evasion**         | Intuitive dodge roll with a single button press        | Quick evasion with dedicated key, precise mouse aim    |
| **Skill Use**       | Comfortable access to multiple skills via face/shoulder buttons | More hotkey options, faster target switching with mouse |
| **Targeting**       | Auto-targeting or soft-lock on nearest enemy           | Pinpoint accuracy for specific targets and AoE placement |
| **Camera Control**  | Right analog stick for fluid camera adjustments        | Mouse for quick, wide-arc camera sweeps                |
| **Comfort**         | Relaxed posture, ideal for extended play sessions      | Ergonomic for precise clicks and rapid input           |
