<Section icon={Target} title="Beginner Guide: First Hour Tactics">

Navigating the first hour of the demo requires a shift in mindset from traditional MMOs. The manual combat system means your survival depends more on your "Spacebar" (Dodge) timing than your gear score. You will start in Goldenhaven and quickly move toward the Amber Coast to establish your first settlement.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Master the Stagger System">
    Bosses have a visible stagger bar. Use heavy skills to deplete this bar, opening a window for massive critical damage.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Equip Skill Gems Immediately">
    Skill Gems are the primary way to modify your active skills. Even the basic gems found in the first 10 minutes can add elemental effects or reduce cooldowns.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Recruit Eslyn">
    Eslyn is your first major companion. She provides essential battlefield support and has 17 unique skills that can be synchronized with your class build.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Start House Upgrades">
    Once you reach the settlement, prioritize building the **Enhancement Forge**. This allows you to upgrade your starter gear using resources gathered from liberated regions.
  </Step>
</Steps>

| Key Control | Action | Tactical Tip |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **WASD** | Movement | Always circle-strafe bosses |
| **Spacebar** | Evasion (Dodge) | Uses SP; do not spam mindlessly |
| **Q / E / R** | Active Skills | Chain these to build Rage or Shade |
| **Tab** | Target Lock | Essential for ranged Stormbringer builds |
| **F** | Interact / Finish | Use on staggered enemies for extra loot |

<Callout type="info" title="Manual Combat">
There is no auto-play or auto-pathing in the **embers of the uncrowned demo**. You must manually aim your skills and dodge incoming projectiles. If you stand still during a raid encounter, you will likely be defeated in seconds regardless of your level.
</Callout>

</Section>

<Section icon={Trophy} title="Progression Systems and FAQ">

Long-term power in Embers of the Uncrowned is driven by the "Infinite Progression" loop. This involves three main pillars: Skill Gem optimization, Gear Enhancement, and Bloodline Awakening. As you liberate the 10 explorable regions in the demo, you will gain "House Glory," which allows you to upgrade your settlement from a humble camp into a fortified city.

| System | Primary Benefit | How to Unlock |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Skill Gems** | Skill modification / Buffs | Dropped by Domain Bosses |
| **Bloodline** | Permanent stat growth | Complete Chapter 3 Main Quest |
| **Settlement** | Better crafting / Trade | Liberate regions and spend resources |
| **Affinity** | Companion exclusive quests | Gift items and fight alongside Eslyn |

<Callout type="tip" title="Domain Bosses">
The demo features 12 boss monsters. "Domain Bosses" are open-world encounters where you can join other players without a formal party. These are the most efficient way to farm Skill Gems during the Steam Next Fest window.
</Callout>
