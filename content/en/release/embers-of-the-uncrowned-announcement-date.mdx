Combat revolves around the **Stagger and Interrupt** system. Bosses exhibit dangerous skill patterns that can be broken if players coordinate their high-impact abilities during specific casting windows. Successfully staggering a boss creates a burst-damage opening, which is essential for clearing high-level raids and Domain Boss encounters.

<Callout type="success" title="Combat Tip">
Always save your dash (Evasion) for red-telegraphed boss attacks. Evasion consumes SP, and running out of resources during a boss's ultimate phase usually results in an immediate party wipe.
</Callout>
