<Section icon={Target} title="Combat Loop and Manual Controls">

The combat in Embers of the Uncrowned is entirely manual, requiring players to aim their skills and time their dodges. The "Stagger" system is the core of boss encounters; by dealing enough damage or using specific "Interrupt" skills, you can temporarily disable a boss, creating a window for massive burst damage.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Input Setup">
    Choose between Keyboard/Mouse (WASD) or an Xbox-style controller. Ensure your dodge (SP-based) is bound to a highly accessible button.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Resource Management">
    Monitor your HP and MP closely. Executioners must manage Rage, while Spectral Blades track Shade generation to empower their finishers.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Stagger and Interrupt">
    Watch for red "dangerous" skill indicators from bosses. Use interrupt-capable skills to break their cast and build the stagger meter.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Evasion Timing">
    Evasion consumes SP. Do not spam your dodge; save it for unavoidable AOE patterns or "one-shot" mechanics in raids.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="warning" title="Stamina Management">
Running out of SP (Stamina) during a boss fight often leads to immediate death. Always keep enough SP for at least one emergency dodge, especially during the **Domain Boss** encounters.
</Callout>

</Section>

<Section icon={Sparkles} title="Progression: Skill Gems and Bloodlines">

Beyond basic leveling, the **embers of the uncrowned beta** introduces "Infinite Progression" through Skill Gems and Bloodline Awakening. These systems allow you to customize the behavior of your active skills, such as adding fire damage to a physical strike or increasing the area of effect for a spell.

Each class features 28-29 unique skills in the demo. By layering Skill Gems, you can create a build that compensates for your class's weaknesses. For example, a Stormbringer can use gems to add defensive utility to their wind spells, making them more viable for solo play in contested regions.

<Callout type="success" title="Bloodline Awakening">
Once you reach the later stages of the demo, focus on **Bloodline Awakening**. This system provides permanent stat boosts and unique passive traits that define your character's legacy within House Harborwell.
</Callout>
