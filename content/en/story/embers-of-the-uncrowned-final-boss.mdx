<Section icon={Shield} title="Phase-by-Phase Boss Mechanics">

The encounter with the **embers of the uncrowned final boss** is divided into three distinct phases, each introducing new hazards and requiring different tactical approaches. Failure to respect the mechanics of a single phase usually results in a total party wipe.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="The Opening Salvo (100% - 70% HP)">
    The boss begins with predictable melee combos and linear projectile attacks. The goal here is to learn the rhythm of its manual combat patterns. Watch for the purple glow indicating an "Interruptible" skill. Using a displacement or heavy impact skill during this window will pause the boss's momentum.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="The Domain Shift (70% - 30% HP)">
    The boss retreats to the center and begins summoning elven invasion forces. Players must split their focus between clearing adds and dodging environmental lightning strikes. This is where the Stormbringer excels at controlling the battlefield while the Spectral Blade hunts high-priority casters.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="The Uncrowned Enrage (30% - 0% HP)">
    The boss enters a permanent Fury state, increasing attack speed and damage. The arena shrinks, and the Stagger bar becomes much harder to fill. Save your Bloodline Awakening skills for this phase to burn through the remaining health before the party's survival tools are exhausted.
  </Step>
</Steps>

| Mechanic | Visual Cue | Player Response |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Stagger Window** | White bar under HP | Unleash high-impact skills immediately |
| **Interrupt Blast** | Purple circular aura | Use "Interrupt" tagged skills to break cast |
| **Shadow Evasion** | Boss turns translucent | Stop attacking; prepare for a rear strike |
| **Ember Nova** | Glowing floor tiles | Move to the liberated safe zones at the edges |

<Callout type="warning" title="Resource Management">
Evasion consumes SP (Stamina Points). If you spam your dodge roll during Phase 1, you will have zero SP left to escape the lethal Ember Nova in Phase 3. Move manually whenever possible and save SP for emergencies.
</Callout>
