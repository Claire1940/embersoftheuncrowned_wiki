### Core Combat Loop
The game emphasizes manual isometric combat. Effective play involves:
- **Basic Attacks**: Your primary source of consistent damage.
- **Active Skills**: Powerful abilities that define your class's combat rhythm.
- **Evasion**: Uses SP, critical for avoiding incoming damage and repositioning.
- **Interrupt Timing**: Crucial for breaking dangerous boss skills.
- **Stagger Windows**: Create opportunities for burst damage after interrupting or dealing significant damage.

<Callout type="tip" title="Key Binding Optimization">
Map your active skills, dodge, potions, and bloodline abilities to easily accessible keys. Comfortable key placement is vital for manual combat, especially during intense boss encounters.
</Callout>
