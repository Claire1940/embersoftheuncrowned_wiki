<Section icon={Target} title="Core Combat and Control Mechanics">

Combat in Embers of the Uncrowned is manual and tactical. You cannot rely on auto-pathing or auto-combat to clear bosses. Success depends on managing your Stamina (SP) for evasions and timing your interrupts to break dangerous boss abilities.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Map Your Skill Keys">
    The game supports WASD movement and controller input. Ensure your active skills, dodge, and potions are on easy-to-reach keys. Manual combat requires constant movement, so avoid cluttered layouts.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Manage the Stagger Window">
    Bosses have a stagger gauge. By using specific high-impact skills during their attack wind-ups, you can deplete this gauge, leaving the boss vulnerable to a burst damage phase.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Utilize Evasion (SP)">
    Dashing consumes SP. Do not spam your dodge; save it for red-telegraphed "unblockable" attacks. Managing your SP is just as important as managing your health.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Interrupt Timing">
    Certain boss skills can be interrupted. Look for the blue flash or specific animation cues to use your CC (Crowd Control) abilities, effectively canceling the enemy's high-damage move.
  </Step>
</Steps>

| Action | Control (KB/M) | Effect |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Movement** | WASD | Manual positioning and kiting. |
| **Basic Attack** | Left Click | Sustained damage and resource generation. |
| **Evasion** | Space / Shift | Quick dash to avoid damage (consumes SP). |
| **Skill Use** | 1, 2, 3, 4, Q, E | Executes class-specific abilities. |
| **Potions** | R, F | Recovers HP or MP during combat. |

<Callout type="success" title="Combat Mastery">
Always keep an eye on your **Bloodline Ability**. This is a powerful "ultimate" move that can turn the tide of a raid. Save it for the final 25% of a boss's health or when you are surrounded by elite mobs.
</Callout>

</Section>

<Section icon={Sparkles} title="Progression: Skill Gems and Bloodline Power">

The "Infinite Progression" system is what separates a novice from a veteran. In this **embers of the uncrowned beginner guide**, we emphasize three layers of character growth: active skill progression, Skill Gems, and Bloodline Awakening. These systems work in tandem to bolster your combat power.

| Progression Layer | Function | Demo Availability |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Active Skills** | Moment-to-moment combat choices and skill chains. | 28-29 skills per class. |
| **Skill Gems** | Socketed items that add modifiers to existing skills. | Found in raids and high-level field content. |
| **Gear Enhancement** | Direct stat boosts to weapons and armor. | Via settlement blacksmith and resources. |
| **Bloodline Power** | Inherited traits that provide massive passive buffs. | Unlocked through main story chapters. |
| **Companion Growth** | Increases synergy and unlocks companion skills. | Centered on Eslyn in the current demo. |

<Callout type="info" title="Skill Gem Strategy">
Do not ignore Skill Gems early on. Even low-tier gems can add significant cooldown reduction or elemental damage that makes farming much more efficient. Check your inventory frequently to ensure your gear is fully socketed.
</Callout>
