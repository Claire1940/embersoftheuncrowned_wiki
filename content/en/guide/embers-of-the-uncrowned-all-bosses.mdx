<Section icon={Swords} title="Boss Encounter Types and Mechanics">

Understanding the classification of the **Embers of the Uncrowned all bosses** list is the first step toward victory. Each category demands a different tactical approach and character build focus.

### Domain and Field Bosses
Domain Bosses are massive entities occupying specific regions of the Amber Coast and Goldenhaven. Defeating them directly contributes to the growth of your settlement. As you liberate these areas, your camp evolves into a vibrant city, unlocking new research and crafting facilities.

### Raid and Defense Encounters
Raid Bosses represent the pinnacle of coordination. These fights often involve multiple phases and "wipe" mechanics that require players to stand in specific zones or interact with the environment to survive. The Defense of Spring mode adds a layer of objective protection, where bosses attempt to destroy key structures while players manage adds and boss aggro.

| Action Type | Mechanic Effect | Recommended Player Response |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Interrupt** | Breaks dangerous skills | Use "Interrupt" tagged skills during the wind-up bar |
| **Stagger** | Creates damage window | Deplete the yellow bar to stun the boss for 5-8 seconds |
| **Evasion** | Avoids lethal hits | Use Shift/Dodge; consumes SP, so manage resources carefully |
| **Aggro Swap** | Shifts boss focus | Executioners should use taunts to protect Stormbringers |
