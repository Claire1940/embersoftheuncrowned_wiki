Combat in Embers of the Uncrowned is fast-paced and demands active engagement. You cannot simply stand still and cycle through a rotation. You must manage your Stamina Points (SP) for evasion and watch for "Stagger Windows" on major enemies.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Movement and Positioning">
    Use WASD or a controller to stay mobile. In isometric combat, being cornered is a death sentence. Always keep an exit path open behind your character.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Managing SP and Evasion">
    Evasion is your most powerful defensive tool but consumes SP. Never deplete your SP entirely on offensive dashes; always save enough for a defensive roll when a boss telegraphs a red-zone attack.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Interrupting and Staggering">
    Watch for specific blue or yellow flashes on Boss health bars. Using "Interrupt" skills at the right moment will break their casting and build the Stagger meter. Once staggered, the boss takes significantly increased damage.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Skill Chaining">
    Link your active skills to maximize output. For example, a Spectral Blade should generate Shade with basic strikes before unleashing a Shade-enhanced phantom strike for maximum critical hit chance.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="success" title="Combat Tip">
Always map your "Dodge" and "Health Potion" keys to the most accessible buttons (like Spacebar and Q). Reaction time is the difference between clearing a raid and a wipe.
</Callout>

### Skill Gems and Bloodlines
Skill Gems are modular upgrades that you can slot into your active abilities. They can add elemental effects, reduce cooldowns, or increase AOE radius. The **Bloodline Awakening** system is a deeper layer of progression tied to the history of House Harborwell, providing permanent stat boosts that persist across different builds.

| System | Primary Function | Acquisition Method |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Skill Gems** | Modifies active/passive skills | Boss drops and quest rewards |
| **Gear Enhance** | Increases raw base stats | Crafting via Settlement facilities |
| **Bloodline** | Permanent character power | Story progression and legacy tasks |
| **Companions** | Battlefield support / Synergy | Affinity quests and recruitment |

<Callout type="info" title="Companion Synergy">
Companions like **Eslyn** are not just cosmetic. They have 17 unique skills and their own affinity-based questlines. Choosing a companion that covers your class's weaknesses (e.g., a tanky companion for a Stormbringer) is vital for solo play.
</Callout>
